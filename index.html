Military-Grade Security

Exchange security

We host our application software on hardened servers, which means we have taken additional steps to enhance the security to a maximum. Our web & mobile applications are protected with four layers of security measures before granting access to the platforms. We hire security experts to perform penetration tests and to discover potential vulnerabilities for all our applications and products.

Wallet security

We don’t store your private wallet keys. Ever. Your deposit is kept on a wallet that is protected by a firewall and additional layers of authentication. We also store part of the funds on a cold wallet for any unexpected circumstances. All access to these wallets is constantly monitored and logged.

Network and cloud security

With the help of our partners, we defend our network and cloud from DDOS attacks or every new vulnerability that requires our attention. Keeping neyco online and running at all times is our top priority, especially as we operate in such dynamic and volatile markets.

Facility security

Armed security guards, cameras, and alarm systems protect the physical access to all of our servers, day and night. All our employees go through extensive background checks and have permissioned access to protect neyco from any internal threats and vulnerabilities.
